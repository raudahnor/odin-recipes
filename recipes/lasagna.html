Lasagña Recipe

Image credit to @alleksana on pexels.com

Feeling Hungry? We're bringing you the perfect comfort food to satisfy your cravings!

Lasagña is a classic Italian dish that's perfect for any occasion. This recipe will guide you through the process of making a delicious lasagña from scratch. Adult supervisison is adviced for children because we don't want you to burn your kitchen down.

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound bulk italian sausage because you gotta keep it cultural ;)
  • 4 (15 ounce) cans tomato sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Now let's get to the fun part!

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. While the noodles are cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add the ground beef and Italian sausage to the skillet and cook until browned.
  5. Drain the excess fat from the meat and return it to the skillet.
  6. Add the tomato sauce, mushrooms, and seasonings to the skillet.
  7. Simmer the sauce for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. In a large bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, beaten eggs, and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  10. Layer half of the noodles on top of the sauce.
  11. Spread half of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
  12. Spread half of the remaining sauce over the cheese mixture.
  13. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of sauce on top.
  14. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  15. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  16. Let cool for a few minutes before serving.
