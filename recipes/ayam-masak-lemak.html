Ayam Masak Lemak Recipe

Image credit to google.com

Saya orang Melayu. Mestilah kena letak resepi masakan Melayu hehe.

My mum makes the best Ayam Masak Lemak! I should probably include her recipe but this is just an example. Maybe I'll create a website to compile all of her recipes she had written down in her book.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 chicken
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised
  • 15-20 chillies
  • daun kunyit
  • asam gelugor
  • 2 pieces of turmeric
  • salt

Now let's get started!

Instructions:

  1. Blend the chillies, turmeric, and lemongrass together until it forms a paste.
  2. In a pot, add the chicken, blended paste, coconut milk, water, and asam gelugor. Bring to a boil.
  3. Once it starts boiling, add the daun kunyit and salt to taste. Reduce the heat and let it simmer until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
